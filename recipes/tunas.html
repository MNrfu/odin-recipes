Tuna Salad

Description :

Tuna salad is a classic, versatile dish made by combining canned tuna with a variety of ingredients, typically including mayonnaise, diced celery, onions, and sometimes pickles or hard-boiled eggs. The creamy texture of the mayo blends with the savory, flaky tuna, while the crunchy vegetables add a refreshing contrast. Often seasoned with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice, tuna salad can be served on a bed of greens, in a sandwich, or stuffed into a pita. It's a quick and satisfying meal, perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

Ingredients :

  • 1 can (5 oz) of tuna
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1-2 tablespoons Dijon or yellow mustard (optional)
  • 1/4 cup finely diced celery
  • 2 tablespoons finely diced red onion
  • 1-2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped (optional)
  • A squeeze of lemon juice
  • Fresh herbs like parsley

Steps :

  1. Mix the tuna first
  2. Add the vegetables
  3. Add the sauces
  4. Add the seasonings
  5. Leave it to chill (optional)
