Cordon Bleu

Description :

Chicken Cordon Bleu is a classic, indulgent dish that features tender chicken breasts stuffed with ham and Swiss cheese, then breaded and fried until golden and crispy. The dish is often baked to ensure the chicken is cooked through while allowing the cheese inside to melt, creating a deliciously creamy center. Typically served with a creamy mustard or béchamel sauce, Chicken Cordon Bleu is a perfect blend of savory, crispy, and gooey textures, making it a favorite for special occasions or a comforting dinner.

Ingredients :

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 slices of Swiss cheese (or Gruyère cheese)
  • 4 slices of ham (preferably thinly sliced, like deli ham or prosciutto)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (preferably panko for extra crunch)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme or herbs de Provence (optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps :

  1. Season the chicken
  2. Stuff the chicken
  3. Coat the chicken
  4. Fry the chicken in oil
  5. Let the chicken drain
