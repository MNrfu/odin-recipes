Pizza

Description

Pizza is renowned for its versatility, offering endless combinations of toppings to suit every palate

Ingredients

  • 1 Ball of dough
  • ⅓ Cup of pizza sauce
  • ¾ Cup of shredded Mozzarella
  • 3 Tablespoons of pesto
  • Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Make the pizza dough
  2. Make the pizza sauce
  3. Prepare the Cheese
  4. Bake the pizza
  5. Spread the pizza sauce
  6. Put the pizza in the oven

Enjoy your pizza!
