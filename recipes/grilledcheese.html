Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese, is a simple yet sublime creation that holds a special place in the hearts and palates of man.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread Mayonnaise on the bread
  2. Add grated cheese
  3. Put the bread on a low heated pan
  4. Cover the pan
  5. Adjust the heat according to the wanted level of grilling

Enjoy your Grilled Cheese!
