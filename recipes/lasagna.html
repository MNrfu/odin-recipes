Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, a timeless classic in Italian cuisine ,is a culinary masterpiece that captivates both the eyes and the palate. This dish is a harmonious symphony of layers, each one carefully crafted to perfection

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Italian seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Enjoy your lasagna!
