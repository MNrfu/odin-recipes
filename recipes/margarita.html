Margherita Pizza

Description :

Pizza Margherita is a classic Italian dish that embodies simplicity and flavor. Originating from Naples, this iconic pizza features a thin, crispy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves. The combination of the vibrant red tomatoes, white mozzarella, and green basil is said to represent the colors of the Italian flag. Baked at a high temperature, the pizza's crust becomes perfectly golden and slightly charred around the edges, while the cheese melts into a creamy, stretchy layer. A drizzle of extra virgin olive oil adds a finishing touch of richness. With its balance of fresh ingredients and deliciously simple toppings, Pizza Margherita is a timeless favorite that showcases the essence of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients :

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ¾ cup (180 ml) warm water
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup (120g) tomato sauce
  • 8 oz (225g) fresh mozzarella cheese
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1-2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Steps :

  1. Make the dough
  2. Let the dough rest
  3. Prepare the cheese and sauce
  4. Put the toppings on the dough
  5. Bake the pizza
